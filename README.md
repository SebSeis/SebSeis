### About me
PhD researcher in construction management and informatics, developing ontology-based methods for quality inspection planning and quality assurance in construction.

### My Work
- Ontology-based quality inspection planning
- Construction quality assurance
- Semantic modeling with OWL, SHACL, and SPARQL
- Formal representation of inspection and quality requirements
- Development of the OCQA ontology
